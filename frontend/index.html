About the Factory

A Legacy of Sustainable Manufacturing

Founded in 2008, GreenLeaf Paper Plates has grown from a small press unit in Tirupati into one of South India's most trusted paper plate manufacturers. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern automated machinery to deliver plates that are strong, hygienic, and completely eco-friendly.

Our 12,000 sq. ft. plant runs three shifts a day, ensuring high-volume output without compromising on quality. Every plate is tested for grease resistance, structural integrity, and food safety before it reaches our customers.
